How to Read a Floor Plan

Doors & Windows

Lines with arcs show door swings. Parallel lines indicate windows and openings.

Structural Walls

Thick black lines represent permanent structural walls that cannot be modified.

Orientations

North arrow indicates unit orientation for natural light and ventilation planning.

Measurements

Dimensions are typically shown in meters. Room sizes help plan furniture layout.
