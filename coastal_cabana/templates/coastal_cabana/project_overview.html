Real estate arm of Qingjian Group, established in 1952

Qingjian Realty is the Singapore real estate development arm of the established Qingjian Group. With decades of experience in construction and development, they have built a strong reputation for quality developments and innovative design.

The group has consistently demonstrated expertise in creating residential developments that combine modern living with community-focused amenities, making them a trusted name in Singapore's property landscape.
